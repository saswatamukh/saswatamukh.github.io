About me

I am a PhD student at School of Computing, National University of Singapore, under guidance of Prof. Divesh Aggrawal and Prof. Maciej Obremski. Prior to that, I obtained master's degree in Computer Science (2022-'24) and bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Computer Science (2019-'22) from Chennai Mathematical Insitute. During my masters, I was fortunate to have Prof. Amit Kumar Sinhababu as my thesis advisor.

Research Interests: Algebraic and Boolean Complexity Theory, Pseudorandomness, Information Theoretic Cryptography, Algebraic Algorithms.
